About Weather at Glance
Features:

Real-Time Weather Data: Get the current temperature, wind speed, humidity, and more for any location worldwide.

Easy to Use: Simply enter the name of the city or town, and instantly see the weather details displayed on an intuitive card.

Open Source: The core functionality of our app is open source, allowing anyone to understand how the data is processed and displayed. Feel free to contribute or customize the code as per your needs!
Our mission is to provide a simple and reliable tool for everyone who needs quick access to weather information. We're constantly improving the app and adding new features to make it even better.
Thank you for using Weather at Glance.
